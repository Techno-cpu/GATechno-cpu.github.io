Definition:

Radiation is the energy that comes from a source and travels through space or other materials. It can move like waves or tiny particles.

🧲 Types of Radiation:

1.   Non-ionizing Radiation – Low energy, usually safe
➤ Examples:

o   Light from a bulb

o   Heat from the Sun

o   Mobile phone signals

o   Microwaves

2.   Ionizing Radiation – High energy, can be harmful
➤ Examples:

o   X-rays

o   Gamma rays

o   Radiation from nuclear materials
